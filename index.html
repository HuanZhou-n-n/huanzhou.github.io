About me

I am a postdoctor at School of Data Science & Engineering, East China Normal University (advisor: Qiwen Dong).

I received my Ph.D. degree in 2019 from East China Normal University, where I was affiliated with the Database System Group (advisor: Weining Qian).

I earned my bachelor's degree from Sichuan Normal University in 2013.

Interests

My expertise lies in database management systems and Data-Driven Computational Education, specifically transaction processing, logging and recovery, concurrency control and learning evaluation.

Experience

I am enthusiastic to integrate research into real-world systems. Right now, I am responsible for designing and developing the Graduate Intelligence Service Platform (GISP) of ECNU GISP and a analysis and monitoring big data platform. Before that, I was a developer of a distributed transactional database system CEDAR. Its key technologies were validated to be effective by several enterprises, including Bank of Communication, Haier, and Jiangsu Mobile.
